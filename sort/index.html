About Us

Welcome to SortSmart!

With the increasing challenges of waste disposal and environmental impact, our goal is to simplify recycling by integrating smart technology into everyday waste bins. By using machine learning and real-time image processing, we ensure that waste is sorted accurately and efficiently, helping to reduce contamination and increase recycling rates.






What we do?

Towards a Cleaner Future: Making Smart Choices for Waste, Energy, and the Environment
While the desire to adopt cleaner and more sustainable practices exists, the real challenge lies in providing accessible and effective solutions. Our initiative bridges this gap by leveraging technology-driven waste segregation to empower individuals and communities to take action effortlessly.



Our Vision: Waste to Value

We envision a world where waste is a resource, recycling is effortless, and technology drives sustainability. Our mission is to reduce pollution, promote eco-friendly habits, and build a cleaner planet through incentivized waste segregation.
